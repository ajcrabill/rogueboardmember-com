What a governance coach does

Governance Assessment

Evaluates your board's current practices against the Rogue criteria and identifies the highest-leverage change opportunities.

Goal-Setting Support

Guides your board through setting SMART student outcome goals and building the policy framework to pursue them.

Monitoring Infrastructure

Helps build your monitoring calendar so outcome progress stays on the agenda — not just when things go wrong.

Board Culture Shift

Works alongside your board over time to move from adult-focused to student-outcome-focused governance — and keep it there.
